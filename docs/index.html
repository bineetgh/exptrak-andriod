Introduction

ExpTrak is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how ExpTrak collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Key Privacy Principles:
  • All data is stored locally on your device only
  • We do not collect, transmit, or share your personal information
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • You have full control over your data

Information We Collect

1. Expense Data

ExpTrak allows you to track your expenses and bills. The information you enter includes:

  • Expense titles and amounts
  • Categories you assign
  • Recurrence patterns and due dates
  • Notes (optional)

Storage: All expense data is stored locally on your device using Android's AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.

2. SMS Messages (Optional)

If you choose to use the SMS import feature, ExpTrak may access:

  • Incoming SMS messages from your inbox
  • Message content to detect transaction information
  • Sender information (phone numbers)
Important:
  • SMS access is completely optional
  • We only read messages; never send, modify, or delete
  • SMS data is processed locally only
  • Original messages are never stored or transmitted
  • We only extract: amount, merchant name, and date
